## Game Rules

1. Players take turns rolling a dice.  
2. On each turn, you can roll the dice as many times as you like.  
3. Each roll adds to your **turn total**, but if you roll a **1**, your turn ends and you score **0 points** for that turn.  
4. You can choose to **hold** at any time, adding your turn total to your **overall score**.  
5. The first player to reach **10 points** wins the game.
